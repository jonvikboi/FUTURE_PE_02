# UGC Hook Generator Prompt

## Purpose
Generate multiple scroll-stopping hooks organized by psychological trigger for any product.

## Prompt Template

```
You are a UGC ad copywriter specializing in short-form video hooks.

CONTEXT:
- Brand: [BRAND NAME]
- Product: [PRODUCT NAME]
- Price: [PRICE]
- Target Audience: [AUDIENCE DESCRIPTION]
- Main Pain Point: [PAIN POINT]
- Key USP: [UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

TASK:
Generate 12 unique hooks (first 3 seconds of a UGC video ad) organized into these categories:

1. CURIOSITY HOOKS (3) — Create information gaps that make viewers NEED to keep watching
2. PAIN POINT HOOKS (3) — Address the viewer's frustration directly
3. SOCIAL PROOF HOOKS (3) — Leverage authority, numbers, or peer experiences
4. SHOCK VALUE HOOKS (3) — Use surprising facts, comparisons, or revelations

RULES:
- Each hook must be under 15 words
- Write in first person, casual tone
- Sound like a real person, NOT a brand
- Never use marketing jargon
- Make each hook feel like the start of a conversation
- Include emotional triggers (frustration, curiosity, FOMO, surprise)

OUTPUT FORMAT:
For each hook, provide:
- The hook text
- Category
- Best platform (Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts / Paid Ads)
- Why it works (1 sentence)
```

## Example Usage (GlowNaturals)

**Input:**
- Brand: GlowNaturals
- Product: Vitamin C Glow Serum
- Price: ₹599
- Target Audience: Women 18-32, active on Instagram, want clean skincare
- Main Pain Point: Dull, uneven skin tone
- Key USP: 100% natural, visible results in 14 days

**Generated Output:**

### Curiosity Hooks
1. "I was about to throw this serum away… then I saw my skin the next morning"
   - Platform: Instagram Reels | Creates irresistible curiosity gap

2. "My dermatologist said I was wasting money on skincare — until I showed her this"
   - Platform: YouTube Shorts | Authority + curiosity combination

3. "Why is nobody talking about this ₹599 serum that outperforms ₹3000 ones?"
   - Platform: Instagram Reels | Price comparison hook creates dissonance

### Pain Point Hooks
4. "If your skin looks dull even after a 10-step routine, watch this"
   - Platform: All | Directly addresses daily frustration

5. "I spent ₹20,000 on skincare last year. Not one product worked. Then I found this"
   - Platform: Instagram Ads | Financial pain + promise of solution

6. "Stop putting chemicals on your face. Here's what I use instead"
   - Platform: YouTube Shorts | Fear-based + immediate alternative

### Social Proof Hooks
7. "50,000 women switched to this serum this month. Here's why I did too"
   - Platform: Paid Ads | Numbers + bandwagon effect

8. "My best friend has the best skin I've ever seen. I finally asked her secret"
   - Platform: Instagram Reels | Relatable story-driven social proof

9. "This serum has 4,000+ five-star reviews. I had to try it myself"
   - Platform: YouTube Shorts | Review-volume social proof

### Shock Value Hooks
10. "I read the ingredients on my old serum. I was literally putting PLASTIC on my face"
    - Platform: Instagram Reels | Shock + educational reveal

11. "Your ₹2000 serum has the SAME ingredients as a ₹200 one. Let me prove it"
    - Platform: YouTube Shorts | Price shock + proof promise

12. "The skincare industry is lying to you. Here's what actually works"
    - Platform: All | Industry expose creates trust
