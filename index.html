The Framework

Proven UGC Ad Framework

Every high-converting UGC ad follows a proven psychological structure. Here's the exact framework we use.

01

Hook 0-3 seconds

Stop the scroll. Use a curiosity gap, bold claim, or pain point that makes the viewer think "wait, what?" The hook determines 80% of your ad's performance.

"I was about to throw this serum away… then I saw my skin the next morning"
02

Problem 3-8 seconds

Agitate the pain point. Make the viewer feel seen. Describe their frustration in their own words — not marketing speak.

"My skin was so dull and patchy, I stopped going out without foundation. I tried everything — expensive serums, DIY masks, you name it."
03

Solution 8-20 seconds

Introduce the product naturally — like you're telling a friend about a discovery. Show, don't sell. Demonstrate the product in use.

"Then my friend told me about this Vitamin C serum by GlowNaturals. It's all natural, no chemicals — and it literally changed my skin in 2 weeks."
04

Proof 20-25 seconds

Show the results. Before/after, close-up shots, testimonials, or social proof. Make the transformation undeniable.

[Show close-up of glowing skin] "Look at this — no filter, no editing. This is literally just 14 days of using it every night."
05

CTA 25-30 seconds

Clear, single action. Create urgency without being pushy. Make it easy — link, DM, comment. Remove friction.

"Link in bio for 20% off — but honestly, at ₹599, it's already a steal. Try it. Your skin will thank you 💛"
